Keep food fresh longer

KALIKA beeswax food wrap, an alternative food storage that is reusable and washable.

Keep bread, veggies, mushrooms, open fruits or left over meals fresh longer. KALIKA lets your food breathe and keeps it from drying out in the fridge.

Good for perishable. Not suitable for raw meat. Do not use to wrap hot food or cover hot containers. Always keep away from heat.

Ingredients: Wild harvest beeswax, cotton fabric, tree resin, and coconut oil.

How to Care

[ TO USE ]
Unfold, wrap, any use the warmth of your hands to create a seal around the object.

[ TO CLEAN ]
Simply wipe or wash in cool water without soap.
To Remove grease, add a little mild soapy water.
Do not wring. Let air dry before storing or reusing.

About

KALIKA Beeswax Wrap was started by Nina Widjaja and Reky Martha as their passionate project. It began as a personal effort to reduce the plastic usage as they witnessed Indonesia becoming the second largest contributor of plastic waste to the world's oceans.

In order to solve this problem, they created a sustainability model for this project and departed as a small enterprise that focused on:

1. Reducing plastic usage by providing innovative product to consumer.

2. Producing high quality alternative food storage using responsible materials.

KALIKA Beeswax Wrap started in January 2018. The wild harvest beeswax comes from Flores and the wild jungle of Sumatra, sourced responsibly by environmentally conscious wild honey collectors maintaining healthy bees life cycle in the forest.
